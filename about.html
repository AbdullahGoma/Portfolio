About Me

I'm an aspiring Full-Stack Developer with a passion for building web applications and continuously expanding my skills. My journey in programming began with an interest in cybersecurity and malware analysis, which eventually led me to discover my love for web development.

My Journey

I started by learning IT fundamentals through Google's Coursera courses and solving algorithmic problems in C++ on Codeforces. After completing a one-month .NET course at ITI, I fell in love with web development and decided to focus on it. Since then, I've been building projects independently to strengthen my skills in both front-end and back-end technologies.
